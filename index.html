About Breadmeadow

Breadmeadow is a cozy rental property nestled in the mountains. We like to think of our B&B as a hub of rest and relaxation: a digital detox where you can disconnect from work, email and social media and connect with who and what really matters. We are in a WiFi-free zone and cell service is limited, so be prepared for a true rustic retreat. Whether you venture out to explore the surrounding area or partake in a winter adventure, or cozy up with a book or a cup of hot cocoa, we hope that Breadmeadow will bring a smile to your face and warm your heart.

Accommodations & Activities

Breadmeadow is an antique farmhouse that dates back to the 19th century. It was built by an earlier settler family who made a living by baking bread for the community. Breadmeadow has four bedrooms, one of which is a top-floor loft, two and a half bathrooms, a cozy living room, and a modern kitchen.

Make breakfast & coffee in our nearly renovated kitchen.

Then, make Breadmeadow your base for a plethora of outdoor activities.

Rejuvenate in the cozy den.

Stroll the town for more adventure!

Finally, come home for the soundest sleep.
